H1N1: A Plan

Disease Profile

  • R0: 4
  • Incubation Period: 1-4 Days
  • Mortality Rate: ~10-15%
  • Transmission
    1. Direct Contact
    2. Contaminated Objects
    3. Inhalation By Contaminated Aerosols
  • Distribution: Adults ages 18-49

Symptoms

  • Quick onset
  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Sore Throat
  • Body and Head Aches
  • Fatigue
  • Vomiting
  • Diarrhea (more common in kids)
