H1N1: A Plan

H1N1 Disease Profile

  • R0: 4
  • Incubation Period: 1-4 Days
  • Mortality Rate: ~10-15%
  • Transmission
    1. Direct Contact
    2. Contaminated Objects
    3. Inhalation By Contaminated Aerosols
  • Distribution: Adults ages 18-49

Symptoms

  • Quick onset
  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Sore Throat
  • Body and Head Aches
  • Fatigue
  • Vomiting
  • Diarrhea (more common in kids)

Focuses

  • Prevention
  • Effective Communication
  • Personal Responsibility
  • Minimal Public Backlash
  • Minimal Public Panic

Course Of Action

1. Educational outreach based on personal responsibility to prevent transmission

2. Advocate for increased vaccination efforts

3. Increase the number of availible health care workers

4. Create resources for rapid burial/disposal of bodies

5. Avoid closing public spaces, cancelling public events, and screening travelers

6. Communicate schedule of information releases to the public and the media
